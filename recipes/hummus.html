Hummus

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (cooked or canned)
  • Tahini
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Blend Ingredients: Add chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, olive oil, and salt to a food processor or blender.
  2. Adjust Consistency: Blend until smooth, adding water if needed to reach your desired texture.
  3. Taste and Adjust: Taste and add more salt, lemon juice, or garlic as preferred.
